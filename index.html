BondServant Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 30, 2025

BondServant is designed for privacy. The app works entirely offline and stores your data locally on your device. We do not collect, track, share, or sell personal information.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No names, emails, or account data
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising identifiers
  • No location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data

Data Stored On Your Device

The following content—if you create it—is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • Serve challenge history
  • Assess (reflection) entries
  • Journal entries
  • Your custom home-screen verse and settings

You may delete this data at any time from within the app (e.g., by clearing history or using the “Reset All Content” setting).

Notifications

BondServant can schedule local notifications to remind you about daily challenges and reflections. These notifications are generated and managed on your device. No notification content is sent to external servers.

Children’s Privacy

BondServant is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Third-Party Services

BondServant does not use any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or crash reporting.

Policy Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date above and, if appropriate, note changes within the app.
