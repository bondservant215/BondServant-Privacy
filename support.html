Common Questions

  • Notifications aren’t firing: In Settings → Notifications, tap Repair Notifications to reschedule reminders.
  • Change the home verse: Go to Settings → Scripture (Home) → set Verse Source to Custom, then enter your text and reference.
  • Privacy: All data stays on your device. See the Privacy Policy.
